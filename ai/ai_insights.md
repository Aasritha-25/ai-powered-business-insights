# AI-Generated Business Insights

This module demonstrates how Generative AI can be used to convert analytical results into clear business insights.

## Input to AI
- Aggregated sales by region
- Sales and profit by product category
- Top-performing products
- Profit contribution analysis

## AI Approach
- The summarized data was provided to an AI model
- The AI-generated human-readable business insights
- Focus was on decision-making and strategy recommendations

## AI-Generated Insights
- The South region shows consistently strong sales performance.
- Electronics outperform Furniture in both sales and profit.
- Laptops contribute the highest profit among all products.
- West region transactions indicate high-value customers.

## Business Value
- Helps management quickly understand performance
- Reduces manual report writing time
- Enables faster strategic decisions

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas)
- Google Colab
- Generative AI (LLM)
